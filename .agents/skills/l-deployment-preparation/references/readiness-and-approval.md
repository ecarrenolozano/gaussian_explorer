# Readiness and Approval

Use:

- `ready-for-deployment`;
- `ready-with-accepted-risks`;
- `not-ready`;
- `blocked`.

Readiness requires identified artifacts, container and data scope, access, configuration, migrations, order, health checks, monitoring, recovery, owners, and decision authority.

Every accepted risk must include description, impact, mitigation, owner, approver, date, and review condition when applicable.

Human approval authorizes the exact plan for execution by an authorized operator or pipeline. It does not mean deployment occurred.
