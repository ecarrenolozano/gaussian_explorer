# Rollback and Recovery

Define:

- measurable rollback or recovery triggers;
- person authorized to make the decision;
- maximum observation or decision period when known;
- application or artifact rollback actions;
- database and data recovery actions;
- configuration restoration;
- dependency and integration recovery;
- verification after recovery;
- communication and incident recording.

The recovery plan must match the actual deployment and migration strategy.

When rollback is unsafe or impossible, document the forward-recovery path, prerequisites, expected duration, and residual risk. Do not label a backup as usable until restoration has been validated or an explicit risk has been accepted.
