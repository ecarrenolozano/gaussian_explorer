# Workflow and Integration Product Overlay

Apply only to externally visible workflow and contract behavior:

- Are roles, permissions, delegations, approvals, rejection, cancellation, reopening, and ownership transitions clear?
- Are configurable business rules distinguished from fixed behavior?
- Are manual intervention, exception handling, audit visibility, imports, exports, and administrative actions represented when required?
- For external interactions, are provider and consumer responsibilities, user-visible success, failure, partial success, duplicate prevention, and reconciliation outcomes clear?
- Are compatibility, deprecation, migration, coexistence, cutover, rollback, and user communication outcomes represented where they affect users or operators?
- Are developer-facing commands, APIs, configuration, diagnostics, and backward-compatibility outcomes specified without prescribing internal implementation?
