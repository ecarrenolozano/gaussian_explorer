# Regulated and High-Consequence Product Overlay

Apply only obligations supported by project evidence. Never guess legal classification or regulatory interpretation.

- Are affected people, protected assets, possible harms, misuse, and required safe or protective outcomes visible?
- Are identity, authorization, consent, confidentiality, disclosure, correction, retention, and audit workflows represented where applicable?
- Are financial amounts, currencies, precision, states, reversals, disputes, reconciliation, and control approvals clear when relevant?
- Are clinical purpose, intended users, patient identity, escalation, human confirmation, contraindications, and domain-review obligations bounded?
- Are alarms, warnings, denial, recovery, and fallback behavior actionable and testable from the user or operator perspective?
- Are specialist review, evidence, approval authority, and applicable standards recorded as project obligations rather than invented story details?
