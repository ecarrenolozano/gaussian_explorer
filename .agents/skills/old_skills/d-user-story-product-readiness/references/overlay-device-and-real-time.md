# Device and Real-Time Product Overlay

Apply to observable device and physical-system outcomes:

- Are device identity, provisioning, ownership transfer, lifecycle, supported variants, and user-visible state transitions clear?
- Are offline, delayed synchronization, intermittent power, degraded sensors, unavailable actuators, and recovery outcomes represented?
- Are timing, deadline, sampling, ordering, or responsiveness expectations defined from an observable event to an observable result?
- Are remote commands, acknowledgement, expiry, duplicate prevention, update, rollback, and recovery behavior clear when required?
- Are safe states, operator feedback, alarms, manual override, and behavior under abnormal physical conditions explicit?
- Are calibration, accuracy, environmental limits, and domain validation expressed as product constraints without prescribing firmware or hardware design?
