# Distributed and Operations Product Overlay

Apply to user- and operator-visible service outcomes:

- Are tenancy, region, residency, availability, degraded-mode, partial-failure, and recovery expectations defined where material?
- Are duplicate work, delayed processing, inconsistent state, conflict resolution, and user-visible retry outcomes bounded?
- Are service objectives, maintenance windows, capacity limits, support hours, escalation, and communication expectations measurable when required?
- Can operators recognize user-impacting failure, diagnose it without exposing sensitive data, and perform documented recovery actions?
- Are backup, restoration, continuity, incident learning, and audit outcomes represented at the product or operational-policy level?
- Are cost, quota, or usage-limit effects visible to affected users or owners without prescribing infrastructure topology?
