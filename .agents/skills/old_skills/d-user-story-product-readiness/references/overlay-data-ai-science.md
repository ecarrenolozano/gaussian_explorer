# Data, AI, and Science Product Overlay

Apply to product meaning and acceptance, not model or data-platform architecture:

- Are data sources, ownership, provenance, units, time meaning, freshness, and relevant quality expectations understandable?
- Are missing, duplicate, corrected, late, invalid, or deleted data outcomes represented when they affect users?
- Are analytical or scientific purpose, valid-use boundaries, assumptions, uncertainty, reproducibility, and domain-expert validation clear?
- For AI or probabilistic behavior, are the supported decision, acceptable error consequences, uncertainty, abstention, human review, and user recourse defined?
- Are sensitive-data rights, consent, explainability, provenance, feedback, and harmful-output concerns represented where required?
- Do acceptance criteria evaluate user or domain outcomes rather than internal model metrics alone?
