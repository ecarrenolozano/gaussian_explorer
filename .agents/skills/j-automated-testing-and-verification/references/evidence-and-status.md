# Evidence and Status

Record separately:

- verification status: `passed`, `failed`, `not-run`, `blocked`, or `not-applicable`;
- evidence type: automated test, static analysis, inspection, external report, or continuous-integration run;
- evidence reference;
- artifact and environment;
- architecture scope;
- known limitation.

Do not convert a blocked or not-run test into a pass. Manual stakeholder evidence belongs to Skill J.
